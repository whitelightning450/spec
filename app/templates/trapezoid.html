Calibration Instructions

Enter Height Above Water:

Enter the height of the camera above the river. It can be entered in feet or meters and must be entered before being able to modify the trapezoid. The live video will only be displayed if a camera height is entered. The camera height will be saved for the current app session and work for all trapezoid pages.

Positioning the Trapezoid:

Move the trapezoid to cover the area of the river from which you wish to obtain velocity measurements. Only this area will be measured. Aim to maximize its size, covering as much of the river as possible while ensuring that the entire trapezoid remains within the camera frame. Experiment with different camera angles and trapezoid placements to capture the desired area for velocity measurement.

Finalizing the Setup:

Once you have positioned the trapezoid as desired, securely tighten the camera. Continue to monitor the trapezoid's position during this process and make any necessary adjustments. After finalizing the camera angle and trapezoid position, click "Save Points" to lock in your settings.

Instructions for Adjusting the Trapezoid:

  • Moving the Trapezoid: Moving the Top Shift slider will move the top of the trapezoid up and down. Moving the Bottom Shift slider will move the bottom of the trapezoid up and down. Moving the X Shift slider will move the whole trapezoid left and right. Lastly, moving the Width Scale slider with widen or narrow the trapezoid.
  • Tilting the Camera: Tilting the camera upward will cause the angles of the trapezoid to widen, while tilting it downward will make the angles narrower.
